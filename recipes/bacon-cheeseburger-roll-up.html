Bacon Cheeseburger Roll-Up

Description

This new take on the classic Cheesburger will become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 4 slices Bacon cut into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1 container (15 oz.) part skim ricotta cheese
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 8oz Velveeta cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 1 pkg (13.8 oz.) refrigerated pizza crust

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Brown ground beef with bacon and onions in a large skillet on medium-high heat. Drain and then return the meat to the skillet. Add Velveeta and cook 5 minutes or until melted, stirring frequently. Cool 10 minutes.
  3. Unroll pizza dough onto a baking sheet sprayed with cooking spray. Press into 15X8-inch rectangle and then top this with the meat mixture. Roll up starting at one long side. Rearrange if necessary so roll is seam side down.
  4. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until golden brown.
