Instant Pot Chicken Mole

Description

Rich, warm, and well-balanced, this chicken mole recipe has layers of heat, fruitiness, sweetness, and smokiness.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • pinch of sea salt & pepper
  • 1 can (28 oz.) crushed tomatoes
  • 6 tablespoons chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 3 cinnamon sticks
  • 2 lb chicken breasts
  • 6 tablespoons cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Use the sauté function on the Instant Pot or multifunction cooker to brown onion and garlic for 3 to 5 minutes using spray oil. Sprinkle in chili, oregano and sea salt & pepper as the onions brown. If needed, use a bit more oil spray to prevent burning.
  2. Turn off the sauté function. Add the remaining ingredients. Ensure that the chicken breasts are submerged in the liquid. Pressure cook on HIGH for 15 minutes.
  3. After the cooking cycle has completed, release the pressure from the Instant Pot, remove the chicken and add to a separate bowl and shred with forks. Fold in the chicken and season to taste with sea salt & pepper.
