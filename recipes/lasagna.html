Lasagna

Description

This is a basic lasagna recipe. Don't let basic get you down, it is still delicious!"

Ingredients

  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 1 container (15 oz.) part skim ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 1/2 cups Shredded low moisture mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 can (28 oz.) crushed tomatoes with basil
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 lasagna noodles, uncooked

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Brown meat in a large skillet on medium-high heat. Meanwhile, combine egg, ricotta parsley, 1-1/4 cups mozzarella and 1/4 cup parmesan.
  3. Drain meat and return it to the skillet. Stir in the crushed tomatoes. Add 1 cup water to the meat mixture and stir until blended.
  4. Spread 1 cup meat sauce onto the bottom of a 13X9-inch baking dish sprayed with cooking spray; top with layers of 3 lasagna noodels, 1/3 of the ricotta mixture and 1 cup of the remaining meat sauce. Repeat layers twice. Top with remaining noodles, meat sauce and cheeses. Cover with foil sprayed with cooking spray.
  5. Bake one hour or until heated through, removing foil after 45 minutes. Let stand 15 minutes before cutting to serve.
